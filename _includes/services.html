Nordic Infrastructure Services

A Nordic Infrastructure Service is a service fulfilling the following criteria: 1. Complies with EOSC Rules of participation; 2. Can be accessed cross-border (in the Nordics at least); 3. Service provider, e.g. National e-infrastructure, agrees to have it listed; 4. Service delivery is either automated or could be automated.
