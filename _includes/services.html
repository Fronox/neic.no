Nordic Infrastructure Services

Nordic Infrastructure Service is a service fulfilling the following criteria:
1. Complies with EOSC Rules of participation
2. Can be accessed cross-border (in the Nordics at least)
3. Service provider, e.g., National Provider, agrees to have it listed
4. Service delivery is either automated or could be automated
